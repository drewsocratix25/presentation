Business Strategy
AI isn’t an IT project.
It’s a business transformation.
The question we hear most is “what model should we use?” The surprising answer: the business model.
Two kinds of ocean.
BLUE OCEANINNOVATION

New, uncontested market space. No competitors yet.
You win by creating value that didn’t exist.

RED OCEANOPTIMIZATION

Existing, crowded markets. Known competitors, shared rules.
You win by out-executing: cheaper, faster, better.

Every AI conversation you’ll have lives between these two.
AI creates innovation opportunities in markets that don’t exist yet.
And optimization opportunities in the market you run today.

Every AI bet lives somewhere on this line. The only mistake is not choosing.
